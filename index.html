Apples

Apples come in many different Varieties. Four of them will be named in this table. All of the apples listed on the table can be found in Ontario.


Variety Color Taste Growing Season
Ambrosia Bi-coloured with bright-pink blush over creamy-yellow background Crisp and sweet October to March
Crispin Very light-green Tart to sweet taste October to April
Fuji Greenish-pink in colour with white flesh Crisp and tart Mid-late October to early February
Honeycrisp Bright-red against yellow background Juicy and a slight acidic, sweet taste Late September to March
